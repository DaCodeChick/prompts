Maximum-Detail Image Prompt
“Alien Cartoon Conversion”

Use the provided character reference image as the absolute visual identity source for the central character.

Redraw the referenced character as a highly stylized early-2000s gothic science-fiction cartoon character, using an exaggerated visual language inspired by angular alien-comedy animation.

The finished illustration should feature:

aggressively angular character construction
oversized head and expressive facial features
narrow torso and extremely thin limbs
sharp elbows, pointed fingers, and triangular shapes
thick, irregular black outlines
flat, highly saturated colors
minimal hard-edged cel shading
distorted perspective
strange asymmetrical architecture
sickly neon greens, toxic purples, deep reds, and heavy black shadows
frantic, mischievous, slightly unsettling cartoon energy

Do not copy or include any existing characters, uniforms, symbols, locations, logos, or recognizable props from another franchise. The image must remain an original reinterpretation of the referenced character.

Character Identity

Preserve the character’s recognizable:

facial structure
hairstyle and hair silhouette
hair colors and markings
skin tone
eye color
clothing design
accessories
jewelry
fantasy or animal traits
personality
signature color palette
overall silhouette

Translate these features into the exaggerated cartoon aesthetic rather than redesigning or replacing them.

The character must still be immediately recognizable as the same individual.

Facial Design

Transform the face using:

an enlarged, slightly rectangular or wedge-shaped head
large expressive eyes with heavy black outlines
sharply simplified eyelids and brows
a tiny angular nose
a wide, highly expressive mouth
exaggerated teeth when visible
dramatic cheek and jaw angles
extreme facial expression without losing the character’s identity

Give the character a chaotic, suspicious, irritated, overconfident, or mischievous expression that suits their established personality.

Do not make the face conventionally cute, soft, rounded, or anime-inspired.

Body and Pose

Show the character in a dynamic full-body pose.

Suggested posture:

torso leaning dramatically forward
shoulders raised unevenly
one arm bent sharply near the chest
the other arm extended outward in an accusatory or theatrical gesture
knees bent at strange opposing angles
feet planted unevenly
head tilted slightly to one side
expression suggesting they are shouting, plotting, or delivering an absurdly intense speech

The pose should feel elastic, theatrical, awkward, and energetic.

Every visible hand must contain exactly four fingers and one thumb.

Clothing Conversion

Preserve the original outfit’s recognizable design language, but simplify it into bold graphic shapes.

Use:

large geometric clothing panels
pointed shoulders
narrow sleeves
oversized boots or shoes
simple flat color blocks
thick black separation lines
exaggerated collars, cuffs, belts, or coat tails
very limited fabric texture
sharp silhouettes rather than realistic folds

Any decorative details should become strange symbols, geometric seams, or angular graphic accents while remaining faithful to the original costume.

Environment

Place the character inside an original warped alien city or laboratory environment.

Include:

crooked walls leaning at impossible angles
narrow mechanical towers
uneven windows glowing toxic green
bent pipes and exposed cables
bizarre control panels
oversized switches and blinking lights
distorted doorways
strange hovering machines
dramatic shadows cutting across the environment
a distant sky in dark violet, dirty red, or acidic green

The environment should feel claustrophobic, theatrical, bizarre, and darkly comedic.

Do not include any recognizable franchise locations or technology.

Linework and Rendering

Use:

thick black contour lines
deliberately uneven line weight
sharp corners instead of smooth curves
graphic black shadow shapes
flat cel colors
one or two hard-edged shadow tones
almost no gradients
no realistic rendering
no painterly brushwork
no soft airbrushing
no photorealistic textures

The image should resemble a polished frame from an original gothic alien cartoon rather than fan art copied from a specific episode.

Color and Lighting

Use a hostile, high-contrast palette dominated by:

toxic lime green
dark violet
crimson red
dirty yellow
cold blue
pale gray
deep black

Light the character from below or from one side using an unnatural green or purple glow.

Use large graphic shadow shapes across the face and body to heighten the character’s dramatic expression.

Composition

Use a slightly tilted camera angle with exaggerated perspective.

Place the character prominently in the foreground, occupying most of the frame. Let the environment bend inward around them, making the entire world feel unstable and strange.

The composition should communicate:

manic intelligence
dark comedy
alien paranoia
theatrical villain energy
chaotic confidence
grotesque charm
expressive cartoon exaggeration
Negative Instructions

Do not:

replace the character with an existing copyrighted character
include recognizable franchise logos or symbols
change the character’s identity
use realistic human anatomy
use anime proportions
create soft Disney-like features
add painterly textures
use photorealism
use 3D rendering
use pointillism
use stippling
use halftone dots
use dotted print textures
add grainy or speckled shading
add extra fingers or malformed hands
create smooth, polished corporate-vector artwork
make the composition calm, elegant, or symmetrical

The final result should be an original, sharply angular gothic alien-cartoon reinterpretation of the referenced character, packed with distorted perspective, expressive posing, toxic colors, heavy outlines, and frantic dark-comedy energy.
