Maximum-Detail Universal Close-Up Eye Banner Prompt

Create an ultra-detailed, high-resolution anime-style close-up eye banner illustration in a wide 21:9 aspect ratio.

Use the provided character as the main visual identity reference. Preserve the character’s recognizable appearance in a general way, including their face shape, skin tone, hair color, hairstyle, eye color family, and overall visual vibe, while transforming the composition into a dramatic extreme close-up focused mainly on the eyes.

The final image should feel cinematic, hypnotic, romantic, magical, and visually intense, with the eyes acting as the overwhelming focal point.

Core Composition

Create a tight extreme close-up crop of the character’s face so the image is framed like a dramatic banner.

The composition should primarily show:

both eyes
eyebrows
the upper bridge of the nose
a small portion of the upper cheeks
strands of hair framing the face from both sides

The face should fill the frame as much as possible.
The image should feel intimate and intense, as if the viewer is being pulled directly into the character’s gaze.

Keep the framing very close and visually striking.
Do not zoom out into a portrait.
This should be a true eye-focused banner composition.

Eyes as the Main Focus

The eyes must be the clear centerpiece of the image.

Make them:

large and visually commanding
wide open or intensely focused
glossy and reflective
highly detailed and expressive
mesmerizing and slightly surreal
elegant, beautiful, and emotionally powerful

Add very rich detail to the eyes, including:

layered irises

subtle inner glow
sharp line definition
elegant shading around the lids

The irises should feel rich, deep, luminous, and visually magnetic.

Heart Pupil / Love Symbol Effect

Inside each eye, place a distinct glowing heart-shaped pupil or heart-shaped iris symbol.

The hearts should be:

centered clearly inside the irises

magical and eye-catching

bright without flattening the rest of the iris detail

The heart effect should feel like the character’s gaze has been transformed into a supernatural expression of affection, obsession, enchantment, or irresistible emotional pull.

The heart shapes should look radiant and alive, as if emotion itself is glowing from inside the eyes.
