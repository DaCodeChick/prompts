The characters should not feel like two separate portraits placed next to each other.

Instead, their silhouettes should visually interlock together to create one unified central design.

Their profiles should nearly touch.

Keep the gap between them extremely small.

The two heads should form a single combined shape when viewed from a distance.

Avoid large empty space between the faces.

Avoid one character floating in the background.
